SBB

Md. Sabbir Ahmed is a Lecturer in the Computer Science and Engineering Department under the School of Data and Sciences at Brac University. He was a Contractual Lecturer for the same department for a year and a half before becoming a full-time Lecturer. During his undergraduate years, he also worked as a Student Tutor in his department for one year and three months. He received his Bachelor of Science in CSE with honors from Brac University. He is now pursuing his Master's degree at the same university. He is a highly motivated learner with strong leadership qualities and a keen interest in research. His primary research interests are Machine Learning, Explainable Artificial Intelligence (XAI), and Image Processing.
